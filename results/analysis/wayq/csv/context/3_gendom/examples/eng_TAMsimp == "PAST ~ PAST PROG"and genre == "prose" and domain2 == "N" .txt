NIV	Within the three days, all the men of Judah and Benjamin had gathered in Jerusalem. And on the twentieth day of the ninth month, all the people _were sitting_ in the square before the house of God, greatly distressed by the occasion and because of the rain.
ESV	Then all the men of Judah and Benjamin assembled at Jerusalem within the three days. It was the ninth month, on the twentieth day of the month. And all the people _sat_ in the open square before the house of God, trembling because of this matter and because of the heavy rain.
